# Polaris Grand Prix -- Week 1 Official Announcement

**Date:** April 20, 2026

---

## To All Polaris GP Teams,

Congratulations on being selected as one of the **six teams** competing in the inaugural Polaris Grand Prix.

Your Tarmo5 kits have been distributed. The clock starts now.

### Week 1 Challenge

**Objective:** Complete **3 consecutive laps** (excluding the out lap) on the designated track located on the **2nd floor**.

**Deadline:** **Thursday, April 24, 2026**

This is a mandatory milestone. Teams that fail to complete the required laps by Thursday will be **eliminated** from the competition.

### What Is Expected

In the next four days, your team must:

1. **Assemble the Tarmo5** from your provided kit, including all 3D-printed components, electronics, and drivetrain.
2. **Solder all electrical connections** -- motor leads, ESC wiring, and battery connectors.
3. **Configure your transmitter** using RadioMaster EdgeTX -- channel mapping, endpoints, trims, and failsafe.
4. **Test and tune your car** to ensure it can reliably complete laps without mechanical or electrical failure.

### Skills You Will Need

This week requires proficiency in:

- **Soldering** -- Clean, reliable joints on motor and power connections
- **Parametric CAD (Onshape)** -- Navigating the Tarmo5 model for fit-checking and troubleshooting
- **Hand tools & fasteners** -- Proper screwdriver technique, preventing cross-threading, working with nylock nuts
- **Software tuning (EdgeTX)** -- Transmitter configuration, endpoint limits, and failsafe setup

Detailed instructions, resources, and a build checklist are available at:

**[saikhurana98.github.io/polaris-tarmo5-docs/week1](https://saikhurana98.github.io/polaris-tarmo5-docs/week1/)**

### Run Day Rules (Thursday)

1. Teams rotate through attempts one at a time.
2. The car must complete 3 full laps without stopping or being touched.
3. The out lap does not count.
4. If the car leaves the track boundaries, the attempt is void.
5. No time limit per lap -- just complete them cleanly.

### Final Note

Four days is tight. That is intentional. The Polaris GP rewards teams that execute under pressure. Start building immediately, divide work among your team, and do not wait until Wednesday night.

Full documentation and resources: [saikhurana98.github.io/polaris-tarmo5-docs](https://saikhurana98.github.io/polaris-tarmo5-docs/)

Good luck.

**Polaris GP Organizers**
