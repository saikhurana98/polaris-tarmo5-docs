## What You Need to Learn This Week

You have 4 days. It is ambitious -- that is the point. Here is what every team member must get comfortable with:

### 1. Soldering Basics

You will need to solder motor leads, ESC connections, and battery connectors. Clean joints are non-negotiable -- cold solder joints will fail under vibration.

**Resources:**
- [Soldering Basics for Beginners (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qps9woUGkvI)
- [How to Solder Wires Together (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vL4DuoKGquk)

**Key Tips:**
- Tin both surfaces before joining
- Heat the joint, not the solder
- A good joint is shiny and concave, not blobby

---

### 2. Parametric CAD Design with Onshape

You need to understand the Tarmo5 CAD files to troubleshoot fit issues, modify parts, and understand the assembly. Onshape is browser-based -- no installation required.

**Resources:**
- [Onshape Free Account Signup](https://www.onshape.com/en/education/)
- [Onshape Fundamentals - Official Course](https://learn.onshape.com/courses/fundamentals-cad-design)
- [Onshape for Beginners (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pMWnsHpDlQE)

- [Tarmo5 Onshape CAD Model](https://cad.onshape.com/documents/85ef3692a7037575fb488acb/w/c077c35ebf137c51bdb17e4c/e/1b4d88cd6ed5e929805daf40)

**What to Focus On:**
- Navigating assemblies (zoom, rotate, section views)
- Understanding part relationships and constraints
- Measuring dimensions and checking clearances

---

### 3. Hand Tools & Fastener Basics

This sounds simple. It is not. Stripped screws and cross-threaded nylock nuts will cost you hours.

**Resources:**
- [How to Use a Screwdriver Properly (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_SBGfMZhRd8)
- [Understanding Nylock Nuts (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K1_MbZunjOQ)

**Key Tips:**
- **Preventing cross-threading:** Start every bolt by hand (at least 2-3 turns) before using a tool. If it resists, back it out and try again.
- **Nylock nuts:** These are self-locking. They are supposed to be tight to thread -- that is the nylon insert doing its job. Do not force them with power tools.
- **Screwdriver fit:** Use the correct size. A Phillips #2 is not a Phillips #1. Wrong size = stripped head.

---

### 4. Software Tuning with RadioMaster EdgeTX

Your car will not drive straight out of the box. You need to configure your transmitter to set endpoints, trims, and channel mappings.

**Resources:**
- [EdgeTX Basics for Beginners (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xvDJDPg2Keg)
- [RadioMaster Setup Guide (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7jNbBdUMN-c)
- [EdgeTX Official Documentation](https://edgetx.org/documents)

**What to Configure:**
- Channel mapping (steering, throttle)
- Endpoint adjustment (limit servo travel to prevent binding)
- Trim settings (center your steering)
- Failsafe (car must stop if signal is lost)

## Checklist

Use this as your team's progress tracker:

- [ ] All 3D-printed parts inspected and cleaned
- [ ] Electronics dry-fitted (motor, ESC, receiver, servo)
- [ ] Soldering complete (motor leads, battery connector)
- [ ] Transmitter bound to receiver
- [ ] EdgeTX configured (channels, endpoints, failsafe)
- [ ] Chassis fully assembled
- [ ] Steering and drivetrain tested by hand
- [ ] First power-on test (wheels off ground)
- [ ] First floor test (low throttle)
- [ ] **3 consecutive laps completed on track**
