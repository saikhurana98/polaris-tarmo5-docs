# Polaris Grand Prix

Welcome to the **Polaris Grand Prix** -- a multi-week RC car build competition where six selected teams race to design, build, and tune their own **Tarmo5** 3D-printed RC cars.

Each week brings a new challenge. Only the teams that keep pace survive.

## What is the Tarmo5?

The Tarmo5 is a legendary open-source, 3D-printed RC car designed by **Kris Shellman**. It is a fully 3D-printed 1/10 scale RC car that teams will assemble, tune, and race throughout this competition.
