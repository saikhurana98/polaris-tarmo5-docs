<div class="section-label">What is the Tarmo5?</div>

The Tarmo5 is a legendary open-source, 3D-printed RC car designed by **Kris Shellman**. Fully 3D-printed at 1/10 scale, teams will assemble, tune, and race these machines throughout the competition.
