    <div class="section-eyebrow">Flow of the Event</div>
    <h2 class="section-title">How the <em>Hour</em> Unfolds</h2>
    <p class="section-lede">Each session runs to a tight rhythm. Six teams, the clock, and a track full of crash potential. Here is what to expect.</p>

    <div class="flow">
      <div class="flow-step">
        <div class="flow-num">01</div>
        <div class="flow-content">
          <div class="flow-step-title">Doors Open · 5:45 PM</div>
          <div class="flow-step-desc">Audience seating along the marked perimeter. Teams roll in their cars, complete their final tech checks, and warm up batteries. The track is open for inspection.</div>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="flow-step">
        <div class="flow-num">02</div>
        <div class="flow-content">
          <div class="flow-step-title">Race Briefing · 5:55 PM</div>
          <div class="flow-step-desc">Organisers walk through the format, track limits, and call order. Teams confirm their drivers, spotters, and pit crews. The clock is set.</div>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="flow-step">
        <div class="flow-num">03</div>
        <div class="flow-content">
          <div class="flow-step-title">Runs Begin · 6:00 PM</div>
          <div class="flow-step-desc">Teams take the track in rotation. Each car must complete <span class="pill">5 consecutive timed laps</span> without stopping. Lap times are called live as the car crosses the line.</div>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="flow-step">
        <div class="flow-num">04</div>
        <div class="flow-content">
          <div class="flow-step-title">Multiple Attempts</div>
          <div class="flow-step-desc">If time permits, teams may run again to improve their best 5-lap session. The fastest <strong>complete</strong> 5-lap run is the one that scores.</div>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="flow-step">
        <div class="flow-num">05</div>
        <div class="flow-content">
          <div class="flow-step-title">Session Ends · 7:00 PM</div>
          <div class="flow-step-desc">Sharp. Whatever has been timed by 7:00 PM stands as the result. Standings go up on the board immediately after.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="section-eyebrow">Rules &amp; Regulations</div>
    <h2 class="section-title">How <em>Scoring</em> Works</h2>
    <p class="section-lede">Clean, simple, public. Every team races against the same rubric. The track is the truth.</p>

    <div class="rules-grid">
      <div class="rule">
        <svg class="rule-icon" viewBox="0 0 36 36" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round">
          <circle cx="18" cy="18" r="14"/>
          <path d="M 18 8 L 18 18 L 26 22"/>
        </svg>
        <div class="rule-title">Out Lap + 5 Timed Laps</div>
        <div class="rule-desc">Each session is one <strong>Out Lap followed by 5 Timed Laps</strong>, run in unbroken succession. The Out Lap doesn't score — it's there to settle the car. The five that follow are the ones being clocked.</div>
      </div>

      <div class="rule">
        <svg class="rule-icon" viewBox="0 0 36 36" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round">
          <path d="M 4 30 L 12 22 L 18 26 L 32 8"/>
          <path d="M 24 8 L 32 8 L 32 16"/>
        </svg>
        <div class="rule-title">Best Average Lap Time Wins</div>
        <div class="rule-desc">Each lap is individually timed. The team's score is the <strong>average across the five timed laps</strong>. Lowest average wins. Consistency beats raw speed.</div>
      </div>

      <div class="rule">
        <svg class="rule-icon" viewBox="0 0 36 36" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round">
          <line x1="9" y1="10" x2="9" y2="26"/>
          <line x1="15" y1="10" x2="15" y2="26"/>
          <line x1="21" y1="10" x2="21" y2="26"/>
          <line x1="27" y1="10" x2="27" y2="26"/>
          <line x1="6" y1="13" x2="30" y2="23"/>
        </svg>
        <div class="rule-title">Up to 5 Faults Per Session</div>
        <div class="rule-desc">A fault counts toward your budget across the <strong>full session</strong> (Out Lap + 5 Timed Laps). Two kinds of fault count:
          <br><br>
          <strong>1. Track Limit Violation</strong> — all four tyres leave the track surface.
          <br>
          <strong>2. Track Reset</strong> — the car becomes undrivable (stuck, flipped, jammed) and needs a hands-on reset to track position. Counts even if the car never left the track.
          <br><br>
          A <strong>6th fault ends the session</strong>.
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="rule">
        <svg class="rule-icon" viewBox="0 0 36 36" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round">
          <line x1="2" y1="30" x2="34" y2="30"/>
          <path d="M 6 30 L 16 30 L 16 14 Z" fill="currentColor" fill-opacity="0.18"/>
          <path d="M 16 14 Q 26 4, 32 22" stroke-dasharray="2.5 2.5"/>
          <circle cx="32" cy="22" r="1.5" fill="currentColor"/>
        </svg>
        <div class="rule-title">The Ramp Jump</div>
        <div class="rule-desc">If the car <strong>fails to land within the track</strong> after the ramp, you must <strong>restart before the ramp</strong> and re-attempt the jump. The session timer keeps running. The failed jump itself counts as a fault.</div>
      </div>

      <div class="rule">
        <svg class="rule-icon" viewBox="0 0 36 36" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round">
          <line x1="6" y1="20" x2="30" y2="20" stroke-dasharray="2 3"/>
          <path d="M 14 12 L 14 28" stroke-width="2.5"/>
          <path d="M 22 12 L 22 28" stroke-width="2.5"/>
          <circle cx="14" cy="20" r="3" fill="currentColor" stroke="none"/>
          <path d="M 24 20 L 30 20 M 28 18 L 30 20 L 28 22"/>
        </svg>
        <div class="rule-title">Reset From the Last Checkpoint</div>
        <div class="rule-desc">When a Track Reset is required, the car returns to <strong>the last checkpoint marker it crossed</strong> — not where it crashed and not the start of the lap. Markers are spaced around the circuit and called by the marshals.</div>
      </div>

      <div class="rule">
        <svg class="rule-icon" viewBox="0 0 36 36" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round">
          <path d="M 12 18 L 16 22 L 26 12"/>
          <circle cx="18" cy="18" r="14"/>
          <line x1="18" y1="4" x2="18" y2="2"/>
          <line x1="18" y1="34" x2="18" y2="32"/>
        </svg>
        <div class="rule-title">Only the Final Counts</div>
        <div class="rule-desc">Wednesday's <strong>Final</strong> is the only scored session. Monday's Semi Finals run the same format under the same rules — same audience, same pressure — but the time set there does not determine the winner. <strong>The Final decides it.</strong></div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
